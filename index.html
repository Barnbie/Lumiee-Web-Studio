FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?▼
Corporate sites typically take 1–2 weeks. Business sites take 2–4 weeks. Custom projects vary — we provide a clear timeline before any work begins.
Do you include domain and hosting?▼
Yes. Our corporate and business packages include domain registration and hosting setup for the first year. We guide you through everything.
Can we update the site ourselves after launch?▼
Absolutely. WordPress sites come with an easy-to-use editor. We also run a handover session so your team knows exactly how to manage content.
Do you offer payment plans?▼
Yes — 50% deposit to begin, 50% on completion. For larger custom projects we can agree a milestone-based payment structure.
